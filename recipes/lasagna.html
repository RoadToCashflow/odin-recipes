Lasagna

Description

This classic Italian dish is made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese.

Perfect for a family dinner or meal prep for the week.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Marinara sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the noodles according to package directions.
  3. Brown the beef and mix with sauce.
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes. Let cool before serving.
